1. Upload the i2c_Scanner.ino to the Arduino.
2. Open the serial monitor. 
----When it has finished uploading, click on the Serial Monitor button, located at the top right corner of the IDE. This will launch the Serial Monitor in the bottom of the IDE, replacing the console section.
----Check this link for more information: https://docs.arduino.cc/software/ide-v2/tutorials/ide-v2-serial-monitor
3. Every found device on the I2C-bus is reported.
----0x3C OLED
----0x38 Temp
----0x69 Gyro

Reference:

Documentation on i2C scanner can be found here: https://playground.arduino.cc/Main/I2cScanner/
